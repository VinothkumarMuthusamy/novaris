Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our clients

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical website takes 4-8 weeks, while branding projects take 2-4 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your web design service?

Our web design service includes strategy consultation, custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, content management system setup, and 3 months of post-launch support.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical support to keep your digital presence running smoothly.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We can work within your existing brand guidelines or help enhance them. Our team is experienced in maintaining brand consistency while adding fresh creative elements.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, project-based pricing tailored to your specific needs. After understanding your requirements, we provide a detailed proposal with fixed pricing and no hidden costs.
